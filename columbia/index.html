Building solutions for social good

Elixir Labs is a 501(c) nonprofit that builds websites, apps, and IT infrastructure for other nonprofits. Absolutely free.

Strength in numbers

Our team is composed of volunteer engineers from top computer science programs, world-class tech companies, and emerging startups.

How can we help?

Starting and operating a sustainable nonprofit is difficult. One of the biggest headaches can be the technology side of things (including the associated costs). That's where we come in. Our team dedicates our skills and spare time to assist nonprofits that are doing good for the world. We can build you a website, mobile app, CRM, task automation tool...you name it. If you have a project that could use our help, follow the steps below to submit a proposal!

01.Identify your needs


What do you want us to build? Even if you don't have a specific project in mind, are there places in your organization where technology can speed things up or solve lingering problems?


02.Write up a proposal


Write a quick one page document describing the request. It doesn't have to be perfect; just enough detail to give us an idea of what you're aiming for.

03.Submit the proposal


Send us your proposal through our Contact Us form or shoot us an email with the attachment at contact@elixirlabs.org


04.Wait for response


We will try to respond to all requests within 24 hours. Due to the volume of submissions, we will choose projects based on engineering bandwidth.
